# 0.5.1 — 2026-05-17

## Added

- **`caveats: string[]` on `secedgar_fetch_frames`** — populated when the queried period matches `^CY\d{4}Q[1-4]$`. SEC XBRL reports fiscal Q4 as the 10-K residual rather than a discrete quarterly fact, so any filer whose fiscal year ends inside the queried calendar quarter is silently absent from a `CY####Q[1-4]` frame. The caveat names the affected fiscal-year-end bucket with examples (e.g. `AAPL Sep-end, ACN Aug-end, CSCO Jul-end, COST early Sep` for Q3) and points callers at `secedgar_get_financials` with `period_type='annual'` for those filers. Empty array for annual (`CY####`) and instant (`CY####Q#I`) periods, where the underlying facts exist. Surfaced in `format()` as `Caveat: …` lines. Closes [#10](https://github.com/cyanheads/secedgar-mcp-server/issues/10).

## Fixed

- **`EdgarApiService` `byCik` index** picks common stock over preferred-share, debt-security, and multi-class variants when SEC's `company_tickers.json` lists multiple ticker entries against one CIK. New `pickPreferredTicker(a, b)` export resolves ties by: (1) hyphen-free wins over hyphenated (`JPM` beats `JPM-PA`), (2) otherwise the incumbent wins (SEC lists common stock first, so `PRU` stays seated against later `PFH`/`PRH`/`PRS`). Replaces the prior last-write-wins behavior that surfaced `JPM-PM`, `BAC-PS`, `C-PR` in `secedgar_fetch_frames` and `secedgar_search_filings` output. Closes [#8](https://github.com/cyanheads/secedgar-mcp-server/issues/8).
